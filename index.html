Totem related information

Brands have been merged in totem skills.

Ascendancy

Warbringer

  • Answered Call - The Answered Call passive skill allows players to summon an ancestral spirit to help in combat whenever they raise a totem

  • Jade Heritage - The Jade Heritage passive skill grants players the Encased in Jade active skill, which allows them to encase themselves in Jade to serve as protective armor. The duration of the armor depends on the number of Jade stacks accumulated, and they will accumulate every second the active skill is not in use.

  • Warcaller's Bellow - The Warcaller's Bellow passive skill allows players to make nearby corpses explode whenever a War Cry skill activates, granting War Cry skills more offensive capabilities.

  • Greatwolf's Howl - The Greatwolf's Howl passive skill allows players to ignore the cooldown durations of all War Cry skills, allowing players continuous and constant usage.
